# Getting unstuck

*Coming soon.*

This is the checklist you'll come back to over and over. When your
code doesn't work and you don't know why, this is where you start.
The full version will cover the read-the-error-message step, the
narrow-down-the-problem step, the rubber-duck step (explaining your
code out loud to something that isn't a person), and the "Mr. Eric,
my buddy is stuck too" step.

For now, here's the short version: read the error message carefully,
look at the line number it points to, and ask your buddy what they
think before asking Mr. Eric.
